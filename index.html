About Me

I am a research assistant and an M.Sc. student at the department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Middle East Technical University, specializing in the signal processing area.

Currently, my thesis studies focus on near-field MIMO Radar imaging using physics-based deep neural networks, and I am supervised by Assoc. Prof. Figen S. Oktem.

My research interests are computational imaging, inverse problems, optimization, deep/machine learning, and statistical signal processing.
