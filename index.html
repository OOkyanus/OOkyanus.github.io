Okyanus Oral

  • CV

I am a Ph.D. student in the Signal Processing Applications for Communications and Radar (SPARC) research group at the University of Luxembourg - SnT, under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Bhavani Shankar. My research focuses on signal processing for radar-based computational sensing and imaging, with particular interest in fast, memory-efficient algorithms and physics-informed deep learning methods.
